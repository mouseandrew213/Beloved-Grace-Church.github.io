First Love Never Dies

March 12,2024

In times of uncertainty, cling to the unchanging love of God. Remember your first love, the pure and enduring connection with Him. Just as Jesus sacrificed for us, His love remains alive within us forever. So, hold fast to this truth: His love is our anchor, our strength, and our eternal source of hope.

Sunday Worship

Every 10 am

Sunday worship at Beloved Grace Church begins at 10 am, marking a sacred time for congregation members to come together in fellowship and devotion. As the church bells chime, hearts and minds unite in reverence, ready to immerse themselves in prayer, scripture, and hymns of praise.

Resurrection Sunday

March 20, 2024

The Passover lamb in the Old Testament serves as a powerful reminder of God's provision, protection, and salvation. Just as the blood of the lamb spared the Israelites from judgment and brought them out of bondage, Jesus Christ, as the ultimate Passover Lamb, offers us freedom from sin and eternal life. His sacrifice on the cross assures us of God's unwavering love and mercy.

Resurrection Sunday

March 28, 2024

In Jesus, we discover the Unleavened Bread, symbolizing purity and the absence of sin. His perfect sacrifice casts a fullness of grace where condemnation finds no place. Through Him, we stand forever righteous in the eyes of God. Let this truth be your anchor: in Christ, we find freedom, acceptance, and unending love.

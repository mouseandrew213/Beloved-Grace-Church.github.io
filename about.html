About

History

Beloved Grace Church began in the early 20th century as a small gathering in a home, growing into a beacon of faith and community over the years. Through challenges and triumphs, the church has remained committed to serving others, spreading the message of love and grace locally and globally. Today, it stands as a testament to the unwavering dedication of its members and the transformative power of faith, continuing to make a positive impact in the lives of many.
